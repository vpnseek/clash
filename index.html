How to Import Clash Subscription

Step 1: Get Your Subscription Link

Log into your service provider's panel. Find the "Copy Clash Subscription" button and click it! The link will look something like this:

https://sub.justmyrelay.com/api/v1/client/subscribe?token=your_secret_token

Step 2: Feed It to Your Client

Open your downloaded client (like Mihomo Party). Go to Profiles / Subscriptions, click Add, and paste the URL you just copied. Tell it to fetch!
